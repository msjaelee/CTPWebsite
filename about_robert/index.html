About Robert N.
INTRODUCTION

35 years sober in AA, 34 years in ACA, 7 years in CoDA: worked the steps in AA, OA (4th), SLAA, NA and ACA (BRB, Tony A’s). Extensive trauma therapy work, including EMDR and (currently) Somatic Experience – singular focus...healing family of origin trauma.

CALL2PARENT WORKSHOPS

This workshop is derived from an evolving Loving Parent workshop Robert has delivered about 9-10 times, including local Bay Area workshops (2015, 2106, 2017), Denver, Colorado ACA (2018), Bay Area CoDA Local Conference (2017, 2019), ACA Regional Convention in Redondo Beach, CA (2016), ACA International Convention in San Diego (2017) and ACA International Convention in Toronto (2018). Robert also developed/started the Call2Parent The Solution: Becoming Your Own Loving Parent focused ACA meeting in the Bay Area. This meeting format was adopted and is an active meeting in the Denver ACA community.
