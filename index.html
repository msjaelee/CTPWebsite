Transforming Trauma Into Wholeness
Presented by Robert N. from San Francisco
ABOUT THIS ACA WORKSHOP

This workshop has a singular focus: parenting traumatized Inner Child(ren) back to health by transforming inner disharmony into harmonious integration. The program content deeply explores the practical, parenting process that yields tangible and effective parenting skills. The resulting parenting practice is the foundation to enable and transform life-long suffering into happiness.

"Becoming your own Loving Parent" is the core tenet of anyone's ACA recovery. If on your "parenting journey" you are clueless about, curious of, struggling with, desiring to polish, or seeking validation - this workshop is for you!
